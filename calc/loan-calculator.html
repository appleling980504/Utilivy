About Loans 关于贷款

  • ▸ Principal: The original loan amount borrowed
  • ▸ 本金: 最初借入的贷款金额
  • ▸ Interest: Cost of borrowing the money
  • ▸ 利息: 借款的成本
  • ▸ APR: Annual Percentage Rate including fees
  • ▸ 年利率: 包含费用的年百分比利率
  • ▸ Amortization: Gradual repayment through installments
  • ▸ 分期偿还: 通过分期付款逐步偿还

Loan Tips 贷款建议

  • ▸ Even small extra payments can significantly reduce total interest
  • ▸ 即使少量额外还款也能显著减少总利息
  • ▸ Shorter loan terms usually have lower interest rates
  • ▸ 较短的贷款期限通常利率较低
  • ▸ Consider your budget carefully before taking a loan
  • ▸ 贷款前请仔细考虑您的预算
  • ▸ Compare offers from multiple lenders
  • ▸ 比较多家贷款机构的报价
