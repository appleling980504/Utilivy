About Percentages 关于百分比

  • Percentage of: Finds X% of a given number Y
  • 百分比计算: 计算数字 Y 的 X%
  • Percentage change: Calculates increase/decrease from X to Y
  • 百分比变化: 计算从 X 到 Y 的增长/减少
  • What percent: Determines what percent X is of Y
  • 百分比关系: 确定 X 是 Y 的百分之几

Math Tips 数学技巧

  • ▸ To find 10%, simply move the decimal one place left
  • ▸ 计算10%只需将小数点左移一位
  • ▸ For 5%, find 10% then divide by 2
  • ▸ 计算5%可以先算10%再除以2
  • ▸ Percentage increase = (New - Original)/Original × 100
  • ▸ 增长百分比 = (新值 - 原值)/原值 × 100
  • ▸ X% of Y is the same as Y% of X
  • ▸ X%的Y等于Y%的X
