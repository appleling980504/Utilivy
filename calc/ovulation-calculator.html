About Ovulation 关于排卵

  • Ovulation: Typically occurs 14 days before your next period
  • 排卵: 通常发生在下次月经前14天
  • Fertile Window: 5 days before ovulation through ovulation day
  • 易孕期: 排卵前5天至排卵当天
  • Cycle Length: Count from day 1 of period to day before next period
  • 周期长度: 从月经第一天到下次月经前一天计算

Fertility Tips 生育建议

  • ▸ Track your basal body temperature for more accuracy
  • ▸ 跟踪基础体温以提高准确性
  • ▸ Use ovulation predictor kits for confirmation
  • ▸ 使用排卵预测试纸确认
  • ▸ Have intercourse every other day during fertile window
  • ▸ 在易孕期每隔一天同房一次
  • ▸ Consult a doctor if trying for over a year without success
  • ▸ 如果尝试一年未成功请咨询医生
