About Pregnancy 关于怀孕

  • Due Date: Estimated using Naegele's rule (LMP + 280 days)
  • 预产期: 使用内格莱规则计算（末次月经 + 280天）
  • Trimesters: Pregnancy is divided into three 13-week periods
  • 孕期: 怀孕分为三个13周的阶段
  • Full Term: Between 39 weeks and 40 weeks 6 days
  • 足月: 39周至40周6天之间

Pregnancy Tips 怀孕建议

  • ▸ Only about 5% of babies are born on their exact due date
  • ▸ 只有约5%的婴儿在预产期当天出生
  • ▸ Most babies arrive within 2 weeks before or after the due date
  • ▸ 大多数婴儿在预产期前后2周内出生
  • ▸ Schedule regular prenatal checkups with your doctor
  • ▸ 定期进行产前检查
  • ▸ Consider taking prenatal vitamins
  • ▸ 考虑服用产前维生素
