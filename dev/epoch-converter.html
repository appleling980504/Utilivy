About Unix Time 关于 Unix 时间

  • ▸ Counts seconds since Jan 1, 1970
  • ▸ 计算自 1970 年 1 月 1 日以来的秒数
  • ▸ Also known as POSIX time or epoch time

  • ▸ 用于编程和数据库
